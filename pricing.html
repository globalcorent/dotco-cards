Billing questions

What customers can expect.

Is Starter really free?

Yes. Starter includes one published card with core templates, QR and contact saving, social links, basic views, instant updates, and DotCo branding.

Can I cancel a paid plan?

Yes. Customers manage payment methods and cancellations through Stripe. At the end of a canceled paid period, the account automatically moves to Free Starter.

What happens when a paid plan ends?

The most recently used card can remain on Free Starter with core features and DotCo branding. Extra cards and paid-only tools are disabled.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. A customer can begin free, then move to Pro or Agency without rebuilding the card or changing its link.
