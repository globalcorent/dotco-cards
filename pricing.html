Billing questions

What to expect

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Use the secure Stripe Customer Portal from your dashboard. Cancellation can be scheduled for the end of your current billing period.

What happens if my plan ends?

Your account and saved drafts remain available, but public cards are unpublished until a publishing plan becomes active again.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Start with the plan that fits today and move to Pro or Agency as you add more cards or clients.
