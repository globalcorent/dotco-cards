Yes. Starter lets you create and publish one public card free with core templates, QR, contact saving, social links, and DotCo branding.

Do customers need an app?

No. Your card opens in any modern mobile browser. Customers can save your contact details without installing anything.

Can I change my information later?

Yes. Update your card anytime. Your existing link and QR code continue to work.

Can I create cards for clients or staff?

Yes. Agency plans are designed for businesses managing multiple cards, brands, clients, or team members.
